## Added Gameplay Mods

- [microDurability](https://modrinth.com/mod/microdurability "smartCard-inline") - Adds micro durability bars above your hotbar for your armor slots.
- [WAIG: Where Am I Going](https://modrinth.com/mod/waig "smartCard-inline") - Adds a compass to the top of your screen. Can be toggled with F6.
- [SmartBlockPlacement](https://modrinth.com/mod/smartblockplacement "smartCard-inline") - Makes holding right mouse button and moving sideways to place blocks a lot smarter.
- [Quick Menu](https://modrinth.com/mod/quick-menu "smartCard-inline") - Open up a menu with **Z** to access quick server commands/functions.
- [Stack to Nearby Chests](https://modrinth.com/mod/stack-to-nearby-chests "smartCard-inline") - Allows you to press a button to stack or restock from nearby chests within your reach. Also allows you to favorite items so you don’t accidentally drop them.
- [AdvancementsFullscreen](https://modrinth.com/mod/advancementsfullscreen "smartCard-inline") - Makes the advancement screen not tiny.

## Added Backend Mods

- [Entity Sound Features](https://modrinth.com/mod/esf "smartCard-inline") - Allows resource packs to add sound variations.
- [Mod Loading Screen](https://modrinth.com/mod/mod-loading-screen "smartCard-inline") - Shows a custom loading screen when loading mods.
- [Surveyor Map Framework](https://modrinth.com/mod/surveyor "smartCard-inline") - Added in preparation for Antique Atlas updating to 1.21.
- [Modpack Update Checker](https://modrinth.com/mod/modpack-update-checker "smartCard-inline") - Alerts you if there is a modpack update and allows us to have a nicer modpack changelog in-game.

## Updated Mod Configs

- Ping Wheel - Changed default ping radius to 256 blocks. Doesn’t really make sense for people outside your view distance to see your ping.
- Inventory Profiles Next - Disabled IPN’s item favoriting in favor of Stack to Nearby Chests item favoriting
- Fancy Menu - Added a single player button, updated the panoramic background, and cleared some backgrounds so you can actually see in-game when looking at your options for example.
