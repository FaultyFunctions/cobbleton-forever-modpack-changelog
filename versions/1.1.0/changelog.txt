# Added Gameplay Mods

- [https://modrinth.com/mod/microdurability](https://modrinth.com/mod/microdurability "smartCard-inline") - Adds micro durability bars above your hotbar for your armor slots.
- [https://modrinth.com/mod/waig](https://modrinth.com/mod/waig "smartCard-inline") - Adds a compass to the top of your screen. Can be toggled with F6.
- [https://modrinth.com/mod/smartblockplacement](https://modrinth.com/mod/smartblockplacement "smartCard-inline") - Makes holding right mouse button and moving sideways to place blocks a lot smarter.
- [https://modrinth.com/mod/quick-menu](https://modrinth.com/mod/quick-menu "smartCard-inline") - Open up a menu with **Z** to access quick server commands/functions.
- [https://modrinth.com/mod/stack-to-nearby-chests](https://modrinth.com/mod/stack-to-nearby-chests "smartCard-inline") - Allows you to press a button to stack or restock from nearby chests within your reach. Also allows you to favorite items so you donâ€™t accidentally drop them.
- [https://modrinth.com/mod/advancementsfullscreen](https://modrinth.com/mod/advancementsfullscreen "smartCard-inline") - Makes the advancement screen not tiny.
