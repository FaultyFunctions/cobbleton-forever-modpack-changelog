## Important

- Make sure you go to Options → Profiles → Cobbleton Keybinds & Packs → Load if you are updating your modpack. This will ensure you have the latest keybinds for the mods added this version.

## Changes

- Updated modpack to Minecraft 1.21.1.

## Added

- [Inventory Scale](https://modrinth.com/mod/inventoryscale) - Be able to change your inventory UI scale separately from everything else
- [Chest Search Bar](https://modrinth.com/mod/chest-search-bar) - Adds a search bar to chests
- [What Are They Up To](https://modrinth.com/mod/what-are-they-up-to) - Now you see if players are typing, in their inventory, or idle!
- [Easy Item List](https://modrinth.com/mod/easy-item-list) - Now you can see the server’s custom items in the EMI recipe viewer!
- [Wild Tool Access](https://modrinth.com/mod/wta) - Press Y to open up a BOTW tool selection menu. Left-click to select.
- [Subtle Effects](https://modrinth.com/mod/subtle-effects) - Fun ambient effects
- [Hard Cover](https://modrinth.com/mod/hardcover) - Adds a few vanilla-adjacent QOL tweaks to the Recipe Book.
